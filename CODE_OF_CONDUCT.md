# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

CerebreX is built by developers, for developers — across every background, identity, and geography. We are committed to making this a welcoming, respectful, and harassment-free community for everyone.

We especially celebrate and uplift contributors from communities historically underrepresented in tech.

## Our Standards

**We expect:**
- Respectful, constructive communication
- Welcoming language toward people of all backgrounds
- Graceful acceptance of feedback
- Focus on what is best for the community
- Showing empathy toward other community members

**We do not tolerate:**
- Harassment, hate speech, or discriminatory language of any kind
- Personal attacks or insults
- Trolling or deliberately disruptive behavior
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Racism, sexism, homophobia, transphobia, or any other form of bigotry

## Enforcement

Violations can be reported to: **conduct@arealcool.site**

Reports are reviewed within 48 hours. Consequences range from a warning to a permanent ban depending on severity.
