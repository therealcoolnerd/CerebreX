## Commercial License (CerebreX Cloud)

The hosted CerebreX Cloud platform at `cerebrex.dev` is a separate commercial product governed by the [CerebreX Terms of Service](https://cerebrex.dev/terms).

Cloud tiers include features not available in the self-hosted version:
- Managed cloud trace storage with team sharing
- Cloud-hosted MEMEX with global Cloudflare edge
- Private registry with access controls
- SLA-backed uptime and support
- HIVE cloud (managed Durable Objects)
